Memory Match

Click one tile to begin the game. Now, click another tile. If the letters on those two tiles match, you've found a pair and the tiles will remain visible. Great work! If the letters on the tiles do not match, you have not found a pair and the tiles will flip over to hide their letters. Try to remember what tiles hide what letters. Continue clicking tiles until all pairs have been found and all tile letters are visible. You can do it!
